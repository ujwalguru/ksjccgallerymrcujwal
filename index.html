Frequently Asked Questions

What is TALAASH? +
TALAASH is an annual event organized by the Karnataka Sanghas Junior College of Commerce, offering a range of competitions and activities.
How can I register for events? +
You can register for events by clicking the "Register" button on the event page or contacting the event coordinators.
What are the event dates? +
The events are scheduled on Day 1 (December 10) and Day 2 (December 11).
Where can I download event schedules? +
You can download the event schedules and guidelines from the "Download PDF" button provided after the event details section.
